Bio

Born and raised in London (UK), currently living in Copenhagen (Denmark). Educated at Royal Central School of Speech and Drama, gradutating from the prestigious performace arts course. Currently studying multimedia design at Zealand institute of business and technology. I'm current co-artistic director of Hence, a theatre company specialising in new writing. In my role as co-artistic director, I am responsible for artistic direction, communication & development of the company.



My other clients were Vernal Theatre Company and Continuum Festival.
